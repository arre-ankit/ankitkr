Welcome to my new website! I've been wanting to create a clean, minimal space to share my thoughts and ideas for a while now, and I finally got around to building it.

## Why I Built This

I wanted a personal corner of the internet that feels calm and thoughtful. Somewhere I could:

- Share quick updates and thoughts
- Write longer essays when inspiration strikes
- Showcase the projects I'm working on
- Connect with others who share similar interests

## The Design Philosophy

This site is inspired by the minimalist aesthetic of [leerob.com](https://leerob.com). I love the generous whitespace, clean typography, and focus on content over flashy design elements.

The tech stack is simple too:

- **Astro** for static site generation
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling
- **MDX** for rich content with components
- System fonts for that native feel

## What's Next

I'm planning to write more regularly about:

- Things I'm learning about web development
- Design decisions and user experience
- Personal projects and experiments
- Books, articles, and ideas that resonate with me

Thanks for visiting, and I hope you'll stick around!
